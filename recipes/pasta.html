Creamy Garlic Chicken Pasta — A Comfort Classic

This dish is the ultimate comfort food for pasta lovers. Imagine tender, golden-seared chicken strips simmered gently in a luscious garlic cream sauce, rich with the bold, nutty flavor of Parmesan cheese. The aroma of sautéed garlic fills your kitchen as the sauce thickens and wraps itself around every strand of pasta like a silky hug. Balanced with just the right kick of black pepper and a hint of chili flakes, this one-pan wonder is both indulgent and simple, perfect for weeknight dinners or casual gatherings. Whether you're eating solo or serving guests, this creamy garlic chicken pasta promises pure satisfaction in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 200g pasta (any kind)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and chili flakes
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta and set aside.
  2. In a pan, cook chicken in olive oil with salt & pepper.
  3. Add garlic, cook until fragrant.
  4. Pour in cream, simmer, then stir in Parmesan.
  5. Add cooked pasta and mix well. Garnish with parsley and chili flakes.
