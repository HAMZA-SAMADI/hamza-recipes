Butter-chicken Recipe

Description

Butter Chicken — The Creamy King of Curries

Butter Chicken, or "Murgh Makhani," is India’s answer to cozy, luxurious dining. This beloved curry brings together succulent pieces of chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked until lightly charred for a smoky depth. The chicken is then simmered in a velvety sauce made of tomatoes, cream, butter, and warming spices like garam masala, cumin, and coriander. The result is a beautiful balance between savory and slightly sweet, mild heat and rich creaminess. Each bite is bold yet smooth, deeply spiced but never overwhelming. Served with basmati rice or fluffy naan, Butter Chicken is the kind of dish that turns any dinner into a special occasion — indulgent, aromatic, and unforgettable.

Ingredients

  • For the chicken marinade:
    • 500g chicken breast or thighs, cut into chunks
    • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1 tsp paprika
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric
    • Salt and pepper
  • For the sauce:
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 tbsp grated ginger
    • 1 can (400g) tomato puree or crushed tomatoes
    • 1/2 cup heavy cream or coconut milk
    • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
    • Salt, pepper, chili flakes to taste
    • Fresh cilantro to garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken: Mix all marinade ingredients and let sit for 30 mins (or overnight in the fridge).
  2. Cook the chicken: In a pan, cook marinated chicken until browned and almost cooked through. Set aside.
  3. Make the sauce: In the same pan, melt butter and sauté onion until soft. Add garlic and ginger, cook for a minute.
  4. Add tomato puree and spices. Simmer for 10–15 mins until thickened.
  5. Stir in cream, return chicken to the pan, and simmer for another 10 mins until fully cooked and sauce is creamy.
  6. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice or naan bread.
