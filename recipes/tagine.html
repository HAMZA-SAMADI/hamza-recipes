Tagine Recipe

Description

Moroccan Chickpea Tagine — A Journey Through Spice and Soul

Transport your taste buds to the heart of North Africa with this vibrant, soul-warming Moroccan Chickpea Tagine. Rooted in centuries-old culinary tradition, this stew is a celebration of color, spice, and simplicity. Earthy chickpeas, tender vegetables, and bold spices like cumin, paprika, and cinnamon come together to create layers of warmth and depth. Slow-simmered to perfection, the vegetables melt into the sauce while maintaining their unique haracter. This plant-based dish is hearty without being heavy, aromatic without being overpowering. Served over couscous or with rustic flatbread, it’s more than a meal — it’s an experience of culture, comfort, and connection.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 carrot & 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley to garnish

Steps

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add spices and cook for 1 min.
  3. Add chickpeas, tomatoes, carrots, and zucchini.
  4. Simmer for 20–30 mins until vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve with couscous or bread.
