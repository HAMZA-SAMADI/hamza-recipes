Fresh Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

Fresh Mediterranean Chickpea Salad A vibrant and refreshing salad bursting with color and flavor! This Mediterranean-inspired dish combines hearty chickpeas, juicy cherry tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, finely chopped red onion, and fragrant parsley, all tossed in a zesty lemon-olive oil dressing. It's light yet satisfying—perfect as a side dish or a healthy main course. Simple, wholesome and ready in minutes!

This salad is the perfect blend of freshness and nutrition. The creamy chickpeas provide a satisfying base, while the crunch of cucumbers and the sweetness of cherry tomatoes add a refreshing contrast. A hint of red onion gives it a subtle kick, balanced beautifully by the brightness of fresh parsley and the tangy lemon dressing. It’s a wholesome, feel-good dish that’s as tasty as it is nourishing—ideal for meal prep, picnics, or a quick, healthy bite any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • (Optional) A pinch of dried oregano or crumbled feta cheese for extra flavor

Steps

  1. Rinse the chickpeas: If you're using canned chickpeas, drain and rinse them well under cold water.
  2. Chop the vegetables:
    • Cut the cherry tomatoes in halves.
    • Dice the cucumber into small cubes.
    • Finely chop the red onion.
    • Finely chop the fresh parsley.
  3. Combine the ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, add the chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and parsley.
  4. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and freshly squeezed lemon juice. Add salt and black pepper to taste. (Optional: add a pinch of dried oregano or some crumbled feta cheese.)
  5. Toss everything together: Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients and mix gently to coat everything evenly.
  6. Serve chilled: For best flavor, refrigerate the salad for 15–30 minutes before serving.
